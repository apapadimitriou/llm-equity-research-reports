## Palo Alto Networks (PANW) – Forecast for 2025 Q1

- **Revenue:** Management guided 2025 Q1 revenue at **$2.10–$2.13B** ([www.paloaltonetworks.com](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/company/press/2024/palo-alto-networks-reports-fiscal-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2024-financial-results#:~:text=For%20the%20fiscal%20first%20quarter,2025%2C%20we%20expect)).  Actual Q1 FY2025 (Aug–Oct 2024) revenue was $2.1388B (14% yoy) ([www.paloaltonetworks.com](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/company/press/2024/palo-alto-networks-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2025-financial-results#:~:text=Product%20%20,2%2C138.8)).  We forecast **~$2.14B** (2,138,800,000).
- **Operating Income:** GAAP operating income was **$286.5M** in Q1 FY2025 ([www.paloaltonetworks.com](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/company/press/2024/palo-alto-networks-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2025-financial-results#:~:text=General%20and%20administrative%20%20,215.2)).  Assuming similar expense structure, we use **286,500,000**.
- **EBITDA:** We add estimated depreciation/amortization (~$70M, i.e. roughly one-quarter of FY2024’s $283.3M ([www.sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1327567/000132756724000029/panw-20240731.htm#:~:text=Share,282.6))) to GAAP OI.  That implies EBITDA ≈ **$356.5M** (356,500,000).
- **Net Income & EPS:** GAAP net income was **$350.7M** (earnings per share $0.99) in Q1 FY2025 ([www.paloaltonetworks.com](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/company/press/2024/palo-alto-networks-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2025-financial-results#:~:text=Provision%20for%20income%20taxes%20,%24%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0%200.56)).  We project **350,700,000** net income and **0.99** EPS.
- **Free Cash Flow:** Palo Alto’s free cash flow (CFO minus capex) typically runs ~35–39% of revenue ([www.paloaltonetworks.com](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/company/press/2024/palo-alto-networks-reports-fiscal-third-quarter-2024-financial-results#:~:text=%2A%20Diluted%20non,to%2039.0)).  Assuming ~34% here (discounted for seasonality), we estimate FCF ≈ **$730M** (730,000,000).
