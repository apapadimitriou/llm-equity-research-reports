### Business Strategy & Outlook
Nvidia's competitive edge stems from its dominant position in GPU architecture for AI training and inference, enabling high-performance computing in data centers [1][2]. The company invests heavily in R&D to maintain technological leadership, such as through its Hopper and Blackwell platforms.

Secular trends favoring Nvidia include the expansion of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cloud infrastructure, driving demand for accelerated computing [from X posts, used for sentiment].
