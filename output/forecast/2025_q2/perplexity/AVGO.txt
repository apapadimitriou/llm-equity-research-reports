This growth is driven by increased demand for Ethernet solutions in AI clusters, where Broadcom's Tomahawk switches, Jericho routers, and network interface cards provide essential connectivity infrastructure for hyperscale data centers[10]. The company's networking portfolio benefits from the industry trend toward Ethernet as the preferred protocol for both scale-out and scale-up AI infrastructure deployments.
