### Investment Thesis
Meta Platforms benefits from secular drivers in digital advertising, where global spend is projected to grow at a 10% CAGR through 2028, fueled by e-commerce and social media engagement [5]. The company's core Family of Apps, including Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp, serves over 3.4B daily active users, providing a massive addressable market for ad revenue, which comprised 97% of Q1 2025 totals at $42.31B [1]. Margin profile is robust, with operating margins expanding to 41% in Q1 2025 on cost efficiencies, generating strong free cash flow of approximately $12B quarterly, supporting dividends and buybacks [1][2].

Key product pillars include consumer payments via WhatsApp and new flows like Reels, alongside value-added services such as AI tools (e.g., Meta AI with 1B actives) that enhance user retention and ad pricing power [3]. Monetization vectors focus on cross-border advertising and emerging markets, with ad impressions up 5% YoY in Q1 [1]. Near-term catalysts include Q2 earnings validation of guidance and AI product launches, potentially driving EPS growth above 20% for FY25.

Buybacks reduced shares by 2% YoY in 2024, with dividends initiated at $0.50 quarterly, signaling capital return commitment [6]. Overall, META's network effects and innovation position it for sustained outperformance in a growth-oriented tech sector.

### Company Description

Its footprint spans advertising, virtual reality via Reality Labs, and emerging AI services, with core revenue from digital ads processed through its proprietary platforms.
