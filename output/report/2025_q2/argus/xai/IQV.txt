### Investment Thesis
IQVIA benefits from secular drivers in healthcare data analytics and clinical research outsourcing, with a $100 billion+ addressable market in global biopharma R&D and commercial insights [3]. The company's product pillars—R&D Solutions, TAS, and Research & Development—leverage proprietary datasets to monetize through subscription models, consulting, and tech-enabled services, driving 5-7% annual revenue growth historically [3][6]. Margin profile remains strong at 23% adjusted EBITDA margins in Q1 2025, supported by operating leverage from scale and minimal client incentives, generating $426 million in Q1 free cash flow for potential buybacks [3].

Capital returns focus on share repurchases, with $425 million executed in Q1 2025, reducing shares outstanding by approximately 1% [3]. Near-term catalysts include backlog conversion in Q2, potential M&A in analytics tools, and expansion into emerging markets, where cross-border data flows could add 2-3% to top-line growth [3]. Overall, IQVIA's network effects and data moat position it for sustained FCF growth above 10% annually, justifying a premium multiple.
