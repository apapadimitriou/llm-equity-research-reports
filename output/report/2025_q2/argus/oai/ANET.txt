**Investment Thesis**  
Arista is well-positioned in a secular upcycle of cloud and AI networking.  Its core datacenter/cloud switching business (~40% of sales) benefits from hyperscale datacenter build-outs, while adjacent markets (campus, routing, network security/observability) expand its $70B+ Total Addressable Market ([ph.investing.com](https://ph.investing.com/news/company-news/arista-networks-q1-2025-slides-276-revenue-growth-amid-ai-networking-expansion-93CH-1807751#:~:text=Strategic%20Initiatives%20and%20Growth%20Drivers)).  The company’s Ethernet switching platforms (100–800GbE) and EOS operating system are widely adopted (≈43% port share in 100G+ segments ([ph.investing.com](https://ph.investing.com/news/company-news/arista-networks-q1-2025-slides-276-revenue-growth-amid-ai-networking-expansion-93CH-1807751#:~:text=Arista%20has%20established%20itself%20as,higher%20speeds%20and%20greater%20bandwidth))), and it holds~#1 data-center switching share ([investors.arista.com](https://investors.arista.com/Communications/Press-Releases-and-Events/Press-Release-Detail/2025/Arista-Networks-Inc.-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Year-End-2024-Financial-Results/default.aspx#:~:text=milestones%20such%20as%20over%2010%2C000,share%20in%20data%20center%20switching)). Product innovation (e.g. next-gen switches, WiFi7 APs) and software tools (CloudVision, AVA) provide high barriers to entry and sticky recurring revenue (~15% service attach).  

Financially, Arista combines growth with profitability. Q1 non-GAAP gross margin was ~64% and operating margin ~48% ([ph.investing.com](https://ph.investing.com/news/company-news/arista-networks-q1-2025-slides-276-revenue-growth-amid-ai-networking-expansion-93CH-1807751#:~:text=Arista%20reported%20robust%20financial%20results,ongoing%20investments%20in%20growth%20initiatives)) – among the highest in networking – owing to high-end product mix and disciplined cost control.  Free cash flow is robust; Q1 ops cash ~30% of revenue, up ~95% YoY ([investors.arista.com](https://investors.arista.com/Communications/Press-Releases-and-Events/Press-Release-Detail/2025/Arista-Networks-Inc.-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Year-End-2024-Financial-Results/default.aspx#:~:text=Commenting%20on%20the%20company%27s%20financial,Our%20strong%20balance)). The strong balance sheet (≈$8.1B liquid capital ([ph.investing.com](https://ph.investing.com/news/company-news/arista-networks-q1-2025-slides-276-revenue-growth-amid-ai-networking-expansion-93CH-1807751#:~:text=The%20company%27s%20balance%20sheet%20remains,term%20prospects))) funds aggressive buybacks (offsetting dilution from options) while preserving R&D investment. We note management’s commitment to returning capital: over $2B was repurchased in 2024 (pre-split) ([investors.arista.com](https://investors.arista.com/Communications/Press-Releases-and-Events/Press-Release-Detail/2024/Arista-Networks-Inc.-Reports-First-Quarter-2024-Financial-Results/#:~:text=Stock%20Repurchase%20Programs)), and programs continue.   

Near-term catalysts include continued large cloud orders (Meta just expanded its Arista deployment ([investors.arista.com](https://investors.arista.com/Communications/Press-Releases-and-Events/Press-Release-Detail/2025/Arista-Networks-Inc.-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Year-End-2024-Financial-Results/default.aspx#:~:text=Fourth%20Quarter%20Company%20Highlights))), new AI networking products, and sales into new verticals.  Our long-term thesis assumes Arista can sustain growth mid-teens (%), driven by cloud, AI, and eventual campus/campus security market penetration.  We view Arista’s stock as a hybrid of growth and quasi-value (via buybacks), yielding total returns well above peers.

**Company Description**  
Arista Networks (NYSE: ANET) is a leader in high-performance cloud networking hardware and software. Its products include advanced Ethernet switches (from 10GbE to 800GbE) and routers, paired with the EOS network OS and CloudVision management suite.  Arista serves ~10,000+ customers globally, including major cloud providers and enterprises, and boasts #1 market share in data center switching (≈100 million ports shipped) ([investors.arista.com](https://investors.arista.com/Communications/Press-Releases-and-Events/Press-Release-Detail/2025/Arista-Networks-Inc--Reports-First-Quarter-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx#:~:text=SANTA%20CLARA%2C%20Calif.,quarter%20ended%20March%2031%2C%202025)) ([investors.arista.com](https://investors.arista.com/Communications/Press-Releases-and-Events/Press-Release-Detail/2025/Arista-Networks-Inc.-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Year-End-2024-Financial-Results/default.aspx#:~:text=milestones%20such%20as%20over%2010%2C000,share%20in%20data%20center%20switching)). The firm’s solutions target data centers, AI clusters, campus networks and routing domains, emphasizing programmability, analytics, and low latency. Arista’s business model blends one-time hardware sales with recurring software/subscription revenue.
