## Company Description

Capital One Financial Corporation operates as a diversified financial services holding company headquartered in McLean, Virginia. Originally spun off from Signet Financial's credit card division in 1994, the company has evolved into a technology-forward banking leader serving consumers, small businesses, and commercial clients across the United States. The company's business segments include Credit Card operations (domestic consumer and small business card lending), Consumer Banking (branch-based lending, deposits, and direct banking), and Commercial Banking (commercial real estate and C&I lending)[6][4]. Following the pending Discover acquisition, Capital One will also operate payment network infrastructure serving hundreds of millions of cardholders globally. The company employs approximately 53,900 associates and maintains a significant branch presence in New York, New Jersey, Texas, Louisiana, Maryland, Virginia, and Washington D.C., while offering national digital banking services[4].
