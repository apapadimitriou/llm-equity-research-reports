## Investment Thesis

Synopsys represents the premier investment opportunity in semiconductor design automation, uniquely positioned to capture the convergence of AI acceleration, design complexity growth, and system-level integration requirements. The company's dominant EDA market position combined with the newly acquired Ansys simulation capabilities creates an unmatched silicon-to-systems platform addressing a $31 billion addressable market growing at 11% annually[10]. This integrated offering addresses critical customer pain points around design complexity, time-to-market pressures, and system-level performance optimization that are intensifying with AI chip development.

The secular growth drivers underpinning Synopsys remain robust despite near-term China headwinds. Semiconductor R&D spending continues expanding as chipmakers invest in advanced node designs, custom silicon, and AI-optimized architectures. The company's AI-driven design tools, including DSO.ai and VSO.ai platforms, demonstrate measurable productivity gains of 20-50% in advanced design cycles, supporting premium pricing and market share expansion[10]. The $8.1 billion backlog provides strong revenue visibility while the 85% recurring revenue model ensures stable cash generation for continued innovation investment and capital returns.

The Ansys integration represents a strategic masterstroke that extends Synopsys' value proposition beyond traditional EDA into comprehensive system simulation and analysis. This positions the company to capture incremental wallet share from existing customers while addressing new verticals including automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications. Management's projected $1 billion in long-term synergies appears achievable given the complementary technology portfolios and overlapping customer bases.

## Company Description

Synopsys provides electronic design automation software, semiconductor intellectual property, and system verification solutions essential for designing complex integrated circuits and electronic systems. Founded in 1986 and headquartered in Sunnyvale, California, the company serves semiconductor manufacturers, system companies, and electronics designers globally through its comprehensive platform of design, verification, and manufacturing tools. With the July 2025 acquisition of Ansys, Synopsys now offers integrated silicon-to-systems design capabilities spanning chip-level EDA through system-level simulation and analysis, addressing a combined $31 billion total addressable market across traditional semiconductor and broader engineering applications.
