## Company Description

Medtronic plc is a global leader in medical technology, serving physicians, hospitals, and patients in more than 150 countries through four primary business segments[12]. The Cardiovascular Portfolio encompasses devices for cardiac rhythm management, structural heart interventions, and coronary procedures. The Neuroscience Portfolio includes cranial and spinal technologies, specialty therapies, and neuromodulation solutions for chronic pain and movement disorders. The Medical Surgical Portfolio provides surgical innovations and solutions for respiratory, gastrointestinal, and renal applications. The Diabetes Operating Unit offers insulin delivery systems and continuous glucose monitoring technologies, though this segment will be separated into an independent company.
