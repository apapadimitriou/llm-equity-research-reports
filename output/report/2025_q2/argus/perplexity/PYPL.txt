## Company Description

PayPal Holdings Inc. operates a two-sided technology platform enabling digital payments for merchants and consumers globally across over 200 markets. The company's portfolio includes the flagship PayPal brand, peer-to-peer service Venmo, enterprise solution Braintree, international remittance service Xoom, and point-of-sale system Zettle. PayPal processes transactions through various funding sources including bank accounts, digital wallets, credit and debit cards, and cryptocurrency, serving over 434 million active accounts with $1.68 trillion in annual payment volume[3].
