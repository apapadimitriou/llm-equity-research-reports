## Company Description

The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. operates as a leading global investment banking, securities, and investment management firm serving corporations, financial institutions, governments, and individuals worldwide. Founded in 1869 and headquartered in New York City, the firm provides comprehensive financial services through three primary business segments: Global Banking & Markets, Asset & Wealth Management, and Platform Solutions. The company maintains significant presence in major financial centers globally, with operations spanning investment banking, securities trading, asset management, and consumer banking services.
