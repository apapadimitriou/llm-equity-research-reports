## Company Description

Keysight Technologies is a leading global provider of electronic design and test solutions serving customers across communications, aerospace/defense, automotive, semiconductor, and general electronics markets. Founded through the 2014 spinoff from Agilent Technologies, Keysight builds upon more than 80 years of measurement science heritage dating back to Hewlett-Packard's electronic instrumentation business. The company operates through two primary segments: Communications Solutions Group serving commercial communications and government markets, and Electronic Industrial Solutions Group providing test solutions across automotive, semiconductor, and general electronics applications. Keysight's comprehensive portfolio includes design simulation software, prototype validation tools, manufacturing test equipment, and network optimization solutions used by over 40,000 customers worldwide.
