## Company Description

CME Group Inc. operates as the world's leading derivatives marketplace, providing futures and options trading across interest rates, equity indexes, foreign exchange, agricultural commodities, energy, metals, weather, and real estate. The company traces its heritage to agricultural trading organizations founded in the mid-1800s, evolving through strategic combinations of Chicago Mercantile Exchange, Chicago Board of Trade, New York Mercantile Exchange, and Kansas City Board of Trade. CME Globex electronic trading platform serves clients globally with nearly 24-hour market access, while CME Clearing provides central counterparty services reducing systemic risk. The company serves professional traders, financial institutions, corporations, governments, and central banks across six continents.
