## Company Description

State Street Corporation operates as a leading financial services company specializing in investment servicing, investment management, and investment research and trading for institutional clients worldwide. Founded in 1792 as Union Bank and renamed State Street Bank in 1812, the company has evolved into one of the world's largest custodians and asset managers with operations in over 100 geographic markets[1][5]. The firm serves mutual funds, pension funds, insurance companies, foundations, endowments, and other institutional investors through a comprehensive platform of custody, fund administration, securities lending, foreign exchange, and related services. State Street Global Advisors, the investment management division, ranks among the top asset management companies globally with particular strength in exchange-traded funds including the pioneering SPDR S&P 500 Trust ETF launched in 1993[5].
