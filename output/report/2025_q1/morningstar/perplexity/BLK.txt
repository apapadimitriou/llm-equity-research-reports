### Business Description

BlackRock operates as the world's largest asset manager, providing investment management, risk management, and advisory services to institutional and retail clients globally. The firm manages assets across multiple strategies including equity, fixed income, multi-asset, alternatives, and cash management. Key business segments include investment advisory services generating base management fees, performance fees from outperforming strategies, technology services through the proprietary Aladdin platform, and securities lending revenue[1].

The company serves diverse client types including pension funds, insurance companies, sovereign wealth funds, corporations, financial institutions, and individual investors. Geographic revenue distribution shows 65.7% from Americas, 30.1% from Europe, and 4.2% from Asia-Pacific regions[11]. BlackRock's iShares ETF franchise represents a significant portion of the business, capturing substantial market share in the rapidly growing passive investment market.
